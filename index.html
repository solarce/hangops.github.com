Welcome to hangops.com

hang with other ops (devs, qa, whomever welcome too!)

What's this all about, hmm?

The idea behind #hangops is that a lot of ops folks (and others) are working remotely these days and so #hangops is meant to be a chance to grab a coffee, beer, etc and hang out (virtually)
You can now follow @hangops on Twitter!

When?

#hangops happens every Friday at 11AM Pacific in a Google Hangout. Usually @solarce or @jordansissel will post the Hangout URL shortly before the event.

Topics?

The topics can be something you're working, something you're curious about, or whatever. If you want to request a topic for a specific week, please submit an issue with your idea/topic/etc.
